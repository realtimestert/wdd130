What to You Want to Play Now?

A Short Guide on What to Play

What games can everyone play and what to do at the actual LAN party? This is a problem that has become more difficult to overcome as time marches forward. There are literally thousands of multiplayer games out there and many of them are not free. This section goes into what can be done to have everyone agree what to play during the LAN party. There will be a list of popular (and free!) multiplayer games that can get the ball rolling on decision making. It can suggest that everyone compiles a list of multiplayer games they would love to play, and everyone begins comparing notes. Games are selected and then research is done to determine what needs to be done to have an enjoyable experience. Will there be just one game? How about 5 games? No matter what there will need to be activities planned for things that do not involve sitting hunched over in front of the computer.
